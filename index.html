Tairo Mulani

AWARD WINNING ARTIST

Tairo is an award-winning artist based in New York.

Tairo Mulani’s art "negotiates the cultural terrain between his adopted home in America and his native Kenya, creating collage and photo transfer-based paintings that expose the challenges of occupying these two worlds."
